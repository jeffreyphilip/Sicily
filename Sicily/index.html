Sicily

A Tour of Sicily

Having been a travel writer for many years I have acquired a deep attraction to Sicily, not only for it’s art and history, but it’s people. Having traveled there frequently I am well acquainted with places to stay, places to eat and places to explore. Please join me in visiting this very special area of Italy! Sicily has been conquered, settled, and abandoned by dozens of civilations, from the Phoenicians, Greeks, and Carthaginians in antiquity, to the Arabs, to the Spanish and Bourbons in the Renaissance, and finally (at least nominally), modern Italy. It’s an intricate and violent story that nonetheless left a fascinating legacy. Touring the relics of Sicily’s tumultuous past can sometimes make you feel that you’re visiting several difference countries at once.
